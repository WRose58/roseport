Sloppy joe toast

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1

    • p>2 tbsp. olive oil

    1 large onion, finely chopped

    1 red pepper, finely chopped

    Kosher salt and pepper

    2 cloves garlic, finely chopped

    1 large carrot (about 8 oz.), coarsely grated 1 lb. lean ground beef 1 tbsp. chili powder

    1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

    1 15-oz. can tomato sauce (no salt added)

    1/4 c. sweet relish

    1/4 c. ketchup

    1 tbsp. red wine vinegar

    2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

    4 thick slices country bread, toasted



    Chopped flat-leaf parsley and mixed green salad, for serving

    Step 1

    Heat oil in large skillet on medium. Add onion and red pepper; season with 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper; and cook, covered, stirring occasionally, 7 minutes.

    Step 2

    Add garlic and carrot and cook, stirring, 2 minutes. Add beef and cook, breaking up with spoon, until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle with chili powder and cinnamon and cook 1 minute.

    Step 3

    Add tomato sauce, relish, and ketchup and simmer until beginning to thicken, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in vinegar and Worcestershire. Spoon beef mixture over toast and sprinkle with parsley. Serve with salad if desired.
